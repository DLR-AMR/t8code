◆ bounds_per_level

sc_array_t t8_forest_ghost_boundary_data_t::bounds_per_level

For each level from the nca to the parent of the current element we store for each face the lower and upper bounds of the owners at this face.

We also store bounds for the element's owners. Each entry is an array of 2 * (max_num_faces + 1) integers, | * face_0 low | face_0 high | ... | face_n low | face_n high | owner low | owner high |

int t8_forest_ghost_boundary_data_t::level_nca

The refinement level of the root element in the search.

At position element_level - level_nca in bounds_per_level are the bounds for the parent of element.
